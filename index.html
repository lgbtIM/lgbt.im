About us

We here at lgbt.im are trying to create a safe, fun environment for LGBT people to communicate online. Many LGBT chat sites are filled with people not seeking conversation, and being dirty. We want more than that from lgbt.im, and we're trying to do that. We seek a community of fun people who enjoy conversing.
