Rules

In order to make lgbt.IM a safe, enjoyable experience for everyone, we have a few simple rules to follow:

  1. Be nice. Don't constantly berate or bully someone. It's not nice. No one likes it.
  2. Be appropriate. Yes, we all realise hormones are flowing like water in LGBT chatrooms, but please, keep your dick to yourself. We mean:
    1. Don't cyber in a chatroom. If you must go cybering, please keep it in a PM.
    2. Don't post lude pictures. Whilst chatting in a public room, please keep your clothes on. As lovely as you feel your naked self is, there's plenty of us who don't want to see it.
    3. Don't just sit there, continually asking for "OMG SOME1 PLZ CAM/RP/WHATEVER WIF ME!!!" Not only is it annoying to many of us, but you're not likely to find anyone anyway.

About us

We here at lgbt.im are trying to create a safe, fun environment for LGBT people to communicate online. Many LGBT chat sites are filled with people not seeking conversation, and being dirty. We want more than that from lgbt.im, and we're trying to do that. We seek a community of fun people who enjoy conversing.
